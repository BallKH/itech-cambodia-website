Who We Are

A specialized IT consulting firm and technology implementation partner

iTech Cambodia is a specialized IT consulting firm and technology implementation partner delivering comprehensive IT infrastructure and digital transformation solutions for small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs), large enterprises, and government organizations across Cambodia.

Our team of experienced consultants and certified engineers provides end-to-end technology services, including enterprise network infrastructure, server and storage solutions, virtualization, hyper-converged infrastructure (HCI), cloud computing, microservices architecture, software development, system integration, reporting and business analytics, cybersecurity, managed IT services, ELV systems, and modern data center design and implementation.

We specialize in designing, deploying, and managing secure, scalable, and high-availability IT environments that align with each organization's business objectives — whether modernizing existing infrastructure, implementing enterprise applications, migrating to the cloud, or strengthening cybersecurity.
